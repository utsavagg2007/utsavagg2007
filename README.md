## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🗑️ [Swacch Gaon](https://github.com/utsavagg2007/Swacch-Gaon) — *AI Waste Management for Panchayats*
An end-to-end platform that helps local governments optimize garbage collection. It **predicts ward-wise waste** using Random Forest Regression, **generates optimized routes** with the Haversine formula and vehicle constraints, and **auto-calls drivers** with their schedules via AI voice agents.

`React` · `Tailwind` · `FastAPI` · `MongoDB` · `Scikit-learn` · `Retell AI`

### 🥗 [NutriDive](https://github.com/utsavagg2007/NutriDive-Project) — *AI Nutrition Scanner*
Scan a food barcode and get an instant, **AI-powered ingredient breakdown** — calories, macros, allergen warnings, and healthier alternatives. Turns confusing labels into actionable health decisions.

`React` · `FastAPI` · `MongoDB` · `OpenAI`

### 🖼️ [Image-AI Backend](https://github.com/utsavagg2007/Image-Ai---Backend) — *Production AI Image Backend* · [🔗 Live](https://image-ai-backend-delta.vercel.app)
A clean, deployed Node.js/Express backend powering an AI image application — structured controllers, middleware, and models, shipped on Vercel.

`Node.js` · `Express` · `MongoDB` · `Vercel`
